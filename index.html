Nelson Kelly Briles was a right handed, fast pitching, professional MLB player from 1965 to 1978. Starting his career off at Chico State, Nelson moved on to play for teams such as the Pittsburgh Pirates, Saint Louise Cardinals, Texas Rangers, and the Kansas City Royals.
